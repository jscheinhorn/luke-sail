Is it possible to sail on another boat?

It is possible to rotate during sails as long as each boat has enough experienced crew for their skipper to sail. Please check with your skipper(s).

Why a catamaran?

They have two hulls and don't roll as much as a monohull. Comfort and spaciousness.
